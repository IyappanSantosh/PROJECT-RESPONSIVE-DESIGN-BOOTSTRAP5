ABOUT US

A tournament is a structured competition where participants compete to determine a winner. Clear rules ensure fairness and consistency throughout the event. Players must adhere to the schedule provided, and any late arrivals may face disqualification. Each match or game will follow a predefined format, and participants must familiarize themselves with these specifics before the event begins. Respect for opponents, referees, and organizers is mandatory; unsportsmanlike behavior can result in penalties or disqualification. Teams or individuals should register within the deadline and provide accurate details, as incomplete or false information can lead to removal from the competition. Equipment or gear required for participation must meet specified standards, and any malfunctions or misuse are the responsibility of the participants. Decisions made by referees or officials are final and should be respected. Appeals or complaints must follow the official procedure within the stipulated time frame.
